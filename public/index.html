FAQ

Questions before you start.

Is this financial advice?

No. SignalForge is an educational tool only. It is not financial advice and does not provide personalized recommendations.

What markets are supported?

SignalForge focuses on supported crypto markets such as BTC and Ethereum pairs. Gold, silver, and oil depend on live commodity provider availability.

How do Telegram alerts work?

Connect the SignalForge Telegram bot, choose timeframes, direction, and minimum confidence, then receive alerts when matching crypto setups are detected.

Can beginners use it?

Yes, but beginners should treat SignalForge as an educational scanner and paper trading journal, not as a guarantee or substitute for risk education.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Stripe, including plan changes and cancellation.
